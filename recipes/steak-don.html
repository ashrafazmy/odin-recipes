Chaliapin Steak Don

Description

The dish is exclusively made by S≈çma combined with the Japanese original dish, the Chaliapin Steak and the donburi concept for his first Shokugeki. Although the dish was made with cheap ingredients that can be found in a supermarket, the dish focused on both innovation and balance of the ingredients to fulfill the donburi mantra, "Dons are completed in one bowl."

Ingredients

For 2 people

  • 2 Steaks Beef Sirloin(Supermarket discount

  • 1.5 Onions

  • 2 Bowls of Rice(Donburi Bowl)

  • 1 Tablespoon of Umeboshi Paste(Pickled Plum)

  • Suitable amounts of Salt and Pepper

  • Potato Starch dissolved in water

  • Finely Chopped Spring Onion(Garnish)

Sauce

  • 4 Tablespoons of Red Wine

  • 1 Tablespoon of Soy Sauce

  • 2 Tablespoons of Butter

Steps

Making the Dish

  1. Chop the onions finely. Remove the tendons from the beef and beat it with a meat tenderizer until as flat as possible. Cover meat on both sides with the chopped onion and leave for 30 minutes or more. Remove the onion and add salt and pepper.

  2. Melt 1 Tablespoon of butter in a frying pan and fry the onion used for (1.) until it changes color; adjust the flavor with salt and pepper and move the onion to a dish.

  3. Frying the meat: Melt the remaining butter in the frying pan from (2.) and fry both sides of the meat before taking it out.

  4. Making the sauce: Add red win to the frying pan from (3.) and boil it down while scraping off the meat juice, put the onion from (2.) back in and fry it. Add soy sauce from the pan's border and mix it with everything while adding it to season; thicken it with the dissolved potato starch.

  5. Put the rice mixed with umeboshi paste in the donburi bowl, put the meat from (3.) on top of it, and add plenty of the sauce from (4.), use finely chopped spring onion as a topping and it's complete!
